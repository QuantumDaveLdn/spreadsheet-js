Spreadsheet Quick Guide

How to Use Formulas:

  • Start all formulas with an equals sign (=). Example: =A1+10
  • Refer to other cells by their ID (e.g., A1, B12). Cell IDs are not case-sensitive in formulas.
  • Use ranges in functions:
    • Column: A1:A5 (A1, A2, A3, A4, A5)
    • Row: A1:E1 (A1, B1, C1, D1, E1)
    • Block: A1:C3 (A1 to C1, A2 to C2, A3 to C3)
  • Arithmetic: +, -, *, / (* and / are calculated first).

Available Functions:

Core: SUM(args) AVERAGE(args) MEDIAN(args)
Logic/Filtering: EVEN(args) SOMEEVEN(args) EVERYEVEN(args) HAS2(args) NODUPES(args)
Manipulation/Generation: FIRSTTWO(args) LASTTWO(args) INCREMENT(args) RANDOM(min,max) RANGE(start,end)
